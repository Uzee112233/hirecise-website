Refund Policy

Clear, fair terms for billing and subscription payments.

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective date: June 2026

This Refund Policy explains how Hirecise ("we", "our", "us") handles payments, subscription billing, cancellations, and refund requests. By subscribing to any paid Hirecise plan, you agree to the terms described below.

💡 Before you pay — try it free. Every Hirecise plan starts with a free trial so you can fully evaluate the platform before any charge. We strongly encourage you to use your trial to confirm Hirecise is right for you.

1. Free Trial

Hirecise offers a free trial period on all paid plans. During this period:

  • You have full access to the features included in your selected plan.
  • No payment is taken until the trial period ends.
  • You may cancel at any time before the trial ends and you will not be charged.

If you do not cancel before the trial ends, your subscription will automatically convert to a paid plan and your payment method will be charged. It is your responsibility to cancel before the trial period expires if you do not wish to continue.

2. No-Refund Policy

All subscription payments are final and non-refundable. Once a billing cycle has been charged, we do not issue refunds for:

  • Partial months or unused days remaining in a billing period.
  • Downgrading to a lower-tier plan mid-cycle.
  • Forgetting to cancel before a renewal date.
  • Lack of use — not logging in or not using features does not entitle you to a refund.
  • Dissatisfaction after the free trial has been used.
  • Team members not using the platform after seats have been purchased.

⚠️ Renewal reminder: Subscription renewals are automatic. You will receive an email notification before each renewal. It is your responsibility to cancel before the renewal date if you no longer wish to continue.

3. Exceptions — When We May Issue a Refund

We will consider a refund only in the following circumstances:

  • Duplicate charge: You were charged more than once for the same billing period due to a technical error on our side.
  • Charge after cancellation: You were billed after successfully cancelling your subscription, with confirmation evidence.
  • Prolonged platform outage: Hirecise experienced a verified outage of 72 consecutive hours or more during a paid billing period that we failed to resolve or credit.

Refund requests for these exceptions must be submitted within 14 days of the disputed charge. Requests submitted after this window will not be considered.

4. How to Request a Refund

To submit a refund request, email us at hello@hirecise.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and include:

  • The email address associated with your Hirecise account.
  • The date and amount of the disputed charge.
  • A brief description of why you believe a refund is warranted.

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 5 business days. If approved, refunds are returned to the original payment method and may take 5–10 business days to appear depending on your bank or card provider.

5. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings at app.hirecise.com/settings/billing. When you cancel:

  • Your subscription remains active until the end of the current paid billing period.
  • You will not be charged again after cancellation.
  • No partial refund is issued for the remaining days in the current period.
  • Your account data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, after which it may be permanently deleted.

6. Plan Changes

If you upgrade your plan mid-cycle, the difference is prorated and charged immediately. If you downgrade mid-cycle, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing period — no credit or refund is issued for the current period.

7. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback with your bank or card provider without first contacting us, we reserve the right to immediately suspend or terminate your account. We encourage you to reach out to us directly at hello@hirecise.com — we are happy to resolve any billing issue promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of Hirecise after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy or a specific billing concern, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@hirecise.com
  • Website: hirecise.com/contact
