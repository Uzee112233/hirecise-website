Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective date: June 2026

Hirecise ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of our platform at hirecise.com and app.hirecise.com.

1. Who We Are

Hirecise is an AI-powered hiring assistant platform operated by Hirecise Ltd. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at hello@hirecise.com.

2. Data We Collect

Account holders (recruiters and HR teams)

  • Name, work email address, company name
  • Billing information (processed securely by our payment provider — we do not store card details)
  • Usage data: jobs created, CVs processed, features used, login activity
  • Support communications

Candidates (job applicants)

  • CV / resume file (PDF or Word document)
  • Name, email, phone number, and any other information included in the CV or application form
  • Application responses (cover letter, salary expectation, custom questions)
  • Consent record

Automatically collected data

  • IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Pages visited, time on site, referring URLs
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see our Cookie Policy)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Hirecise platform
  • To process CV submissions and generate AI candidate rankings for the recruiting team that posted the job
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmations, billing receipts, trial reminders)
  • To send product updates and marketing emails (you can opt out at any time)
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or UK, we process your data under one of the following legal bases:

  • Contract: processing necessary to provide the services you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests: improving the platform, fraud prevention, marketing to existing customers
  • Consent: where we ask for your explicit agreement (e.g., marketing emails to non-customers)
  • Legal obligation: where required by law

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • The recruiting organisation: candidate CV and application data is shared with the employer who posted the job
  • AI processing provider: CV content is sent to an AI model API to generate scores and summaries (data is not retained for training by the provider under our agreement)
  • Payment processor: billing information is handled by our payment provider under PCI-DSS compliance
  • Infrastructure providers: cloud hosting and database services under data processing agreements
  • Legal authorities: where required by a valid legal request

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days after cancellation
  • Candidate CVs and application data are retained as long as the recruiting account is active, or for 12 months from submission — whichever is shorter — unless the recruiter deletes them earlier
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@hirecise.com

7. Data Security

We take security seriously. Measures include:

  • AES-256 encryption at rest for all stored data
  • TLS encryption in transit for all data transfers
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Regular security reviews

See our full Security page for details.

8. International Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you are based in the EEA or UK, your data is transferred to the US under appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses). By using Hirecise, you acknowledge this transfer.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@hirecise.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies. For full details, see our Cookie Policy.

11. Children's Privacy

Hirecise is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email or an in-app notice. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent version.

13. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@hirecise.com or use the contact form.
