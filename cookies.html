Cookie Policy

What cookies we use, why, and how to manage them.

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective date: June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Hirecise ("we", "our", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on hirecise.com and app.hirecise.com. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, work more efficiently, and to provide reporting information. Cookies can be "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent" cookies (remaining on your device for a set period).

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
access_token Essential Keeps you logged in to your Hirecise account securely (HttpOnly, Secure flag) Session / 7 days
csrf_token Essential Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks Session
color-scheme Functional Remembers your display preference (light/dark mode) 1 year
_ga, _ga_* Analytics Google Analytics — helps us understand how visitors use the site (page views, traffic sources). Data is anonymised. 2 years
_hjSession* Analytics Hotjar (if enabled) — session recording and heatmaps to improve UX 30 minutes

3. Cookie Categories

  • Essential cookies: Required for the platform to function. You cannot opt out of these while using Hirecise — without them, core features like staying logged in would not work.
  • Functional cookies: Enhance your experience by remembering your preferences. Disabling them may affect how the site looks or behaves.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how people use Hirecise so we can improve it. Data collected is aggregated and anonymised where possible.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use. We do not control these cookies and they are governed by the respective third-party privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — Google Privacy Policy
  • Hotjar (if active) — Hotjar Privacy Policy

5. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to view, block, or delete cookies through their settings. Note that blocking essential cookies will prevent you from logging in.
  • Google Analytics opt-out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Do Not Track: We respect the Do Not Track (DNT) browser signal — when enabled, we disable analytics tracking

Browser-specific cookie management guides:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or law. We will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page when we do. Continued use of Hirecise after changes are posted constitutes acceptance.
