Both plans come with a 14-day free trial that includes up to 50 AI evaluations. You get full access to all features on your chosen plan — no credit card required to start.
After your 14-day trial, you'll be moved to your chosen plan. We send a reminder 2 days before so you're never surprised. If you decide not to continue, simply cancel during the trial at no charge.
Each CV submitted through your career page and scored by the AI counts as 1 evaluation towards your monthly limit. Your limit resets on the 1st of each month. Starter includes 500/month; Growth includes 3,000/month.
On both Starter and Growth plans, your public job listings page can show your company logo and branding. Candidates applying to your roles see a branded experience. Logo upload and career page customization are included on all paid plans.
AI interview questions are automatically generated for each candidate based on their CV and your job requirements. They include technical, behavioral, and role-specific questions. Unlimited on both Starter and Growth plans.
